21st Century Teacher

Teaching, a profession were no one is less but there are some who will stand out in the field. 16 years of spending most of my time in school as a student and an enough time to identify the noble trait that I bestow in some of the 21st century teachers I have met back then. What makes them so special? In what way they standout in most? I believe here are some characteristics of a 21st century teacher that makes them one of a kind;

First step in the room an admirable teacher will put a smile on her face knowing the importance of a smile will create positive vibes to her students and as its simplest but can lighten up the atmosphere. Knows how to make her lesson more engaging by creating different approach because she is aware of the diversity in each of her students, she will accept nor forsake, she will think more neither think less, she will have faith for them until her students will learn how to put faith onto themselves, she will be there to correct her students flaws and mistake but never make them feel insecure about it and instead guiding them to the right path.“No one would ever left behind when it comes to learning” that’s the mentality of an excellent teacher and this will help her find ways that will make her subject more fun and at the same time students will acquire knowledge from it. Great teacher will never ever underestimate her students capabilities instead will encourage them to find their passion and to even cheer for the effort they exert as she’s aware of Multi-Intelligence residing within her students.

By means of teaching a 21st century teacher will know how to make friend with the technology as she is aware of its importance especially in bringing lesson more lively and accessible. 21st century teacher does not just depend on a textbook, she possesses creativity that will bring her student to the peak of their imagination she knows how to connect to her student by bringing the ideas from the text to reality. Excellent teachers are a package of a guidance from developing and improving critical thinking skills of her students as well as building their moral. Not just that, she knows her vital role to the lives of her student and to the society. Thus, she will be devoted to the profession she was in. that make her a 21st century noblest teacher
